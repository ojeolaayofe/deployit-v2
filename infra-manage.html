What is IT Infrastructure and How do you manage it?

IT Infrastructure



Have you ever been to a giant organization and wondered how they keep up efficient communication with an effective measure for good productivity? Well, it all depends on how strong is the foundation of your operational processes. With a good IT infrastructure, a business can achieve greater efficiency and stay on the right path for growth. You can consider IT infrastructure as a medium that allows you to conduct IT-related processes in a systematic order while enabling data traveling within or outside the organization.

Types of IT Infrastructure

Traditional Infrastructure

Components including data storage, data center, and other resources are all managed and operated by the corporation within their own facilities in conventional infrastructure. Traditional infrastructure is also regarded as costly to maintain and requires detailed hardware, such as computers, power, and physical space.



Cloud Infrastructure

It is known to define the cloud computing components and services required by an organization. By creating it using resources, one can create a private cloud. However, leasing cloud assets from a cloud provider like Amazon, Alibaba, IBM, Google, or Microsoft, requires you to use a public cloud.



· Hyper-Converged Infrastructure

The hyper-converged network allows you to handle computing, networking, and data storage resources from a single interface. You can accommodate scalable architectures on industry-standard hardware, with software-defined computing and data storage combined to manage modern workload with sorted work pressure.
